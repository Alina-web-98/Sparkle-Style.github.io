Delivery & payment

Shipping

We at Sparkle and Style are committed to providing fast and trustworthy delivery of your orders. Delivery options depend on your location and the shipping method you choose.

Delivery times
  • Domestic delivery: 2-5 business days.
  • International shipping: 5-10 business days, depending on the country.
Shipping costs
  • Domestic shipping may be free for orders over $500.
  • International shipping is calculated automatically at checkout.
Order tracking
  • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number so you always know where your package is.
Packaging
  • We make sure your jewelry arrives safe and sound, so each piece is carefully packaged in a branded gift box.

Payment

We offer several convenient and secure payment options for your comfort:

Bank cards
  • We accept Visa, MasterCard and other international cards. All transactions are secure and your data is kept safe.
Electronic wallets
  • You can pay for your order through popular e-wallets such as PayPal and others available in your area.
Payment on receipt
  • In some regions, payment on receipt is available, allowing you to pay for your order directly at the time of delivery.
Payment security
  • All payments go through secure systems that meet international security standards, so your data stays safe.
