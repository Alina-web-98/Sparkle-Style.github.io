About us

Sparkle & Style is your trusted partner in the world of exquisite jewelry. The company was founded with the aim of offering our customers unique, high quality and elegant jewelry that emphasizes the individuality and exquisite taste of everyone.

Each piece of jewelry undergoes strict quality control and is accompanied by a certificate of authenticity. We are proud that our products give joy and emphasize the beauty of our customers.

We offer a wide range of jewelry for every taste: from classic models to modern and avant-garde designs.

We believe in combining tradition and innovation. We strive for perfection in every product. At Sparkle & Style, everyone will find something special.

Our team are professionals who share a passion for the art of jewelry.

  • Masters and designers create real works of art using the best materials and modern technologies.
  • Consultants work hard to ensure that every customer is satisfied with their choice.

We pride ourselves on providing an exceptional level of support and service to our customers.

Frequently asked questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including bank cards, e-wallets and payment on receipt. A full list of available payment methods can be found on the delivery and payment page.

How long does it take for delivery?

Delivery time depends on your location and the shipping method you choose. It usually takes 2 to 7 business days for delivery. We provide a tracking number so you can follow your order's progress.

How to care for jewelry?

We recommend cleaning your jewelry regularly with a soft cloth and storing it in a separate box or bag to avoid damage. Avoid contact of jewelry with aggressive chemicals such as perfumes and cleaning agents.

How do you choose a ring size?

For accurate ring sizing, we recommend using our size chart or consulting a jeweler. If you have difficulties in determining the size, our consultants are always ready to help.

Do you provide a warranty on your products?

Yes, we provide a warranty on all our products. The warranty covers possible defects in workmanship or materials. If you have any questions about the warranty, please contact our support team.

How do I contact your support team?

You can contact us by phone, email or via the feedback form on the website. Our support team is always available to help you with any questions or concerns.
